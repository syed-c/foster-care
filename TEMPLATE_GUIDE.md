# Foster Care Directory - Template Structure Guide

This document provides an overview of the template structure used in the Foster Care Directory CMS.

## Template Types

### 1. Country Template
**Path Pattern:** `/foster-agency/{country}`

**Sections:**
1. **Overview of Fostering** - Introduction to fostering in the country
2. **Foster Agency Finder by Region** - Help users find agencies by region
3. **Featured Popular Locations** - Showcase popular regions/cities
4. **Foster Care System** - Information about the country's foster care system
5. **Why Choose to Foster** - Reasons to consider fostering
6. **Frequently Asked Questions** - Common questions and answers

### 2. Region/County Template
**Path Pattern:** `/foster-agency/{country}/{region}`

**Sections:**
1. **About Fostering** - Introduction to fostering in the region
2. **Benefits and Support** - Local benefits and support services
3. **Training & Development** - Local training programs
4. **Popular Cities** - Featured cities within the region
5. **Testimonials** - Success stories from local foster carers
6. **Top Foster Agencies** - Featured agencies in the region
7. **Frequently Asked Questions** - Regional-specific FAQs

### 3. City Template
**Path Pattern:** `/foster-agency/{country}/{region}/{city}`

**Sections:**
1. **Hero Section** - Main hero with call-to-action buttons
2. **About Fostering** - Introduction to fostering in the city
3. **Types of Fostering** - Different fostering opportunities
4. **Top Foster Agencies** - Featured agencies in the city
5. **Why Foster** - Reasons to consider fostering in the city
6. **Allowances & Support** - Financial and support information
7. **Local Resources** - Links to local resources
8. **FAQs** - City-specific frequently asked questions

## Content Guidelines

### General Principles
- Use clear, concise language that's easy to understand
- Include local information specific to each location
- Add relevant links to external resources when appropriate
- Keep FAQ answers helpful and informative
- Use consistent formatting and styling across all sections
- Focus on the needs of prospective foster carers
- Highlight local support services and community resources

### Section-Specific Guidance

#### Hero Section (City Template)
- Create a compelling heading that encourages exploration
- Provide a clear subheading with relevant information
- Include call-to-action buttons that guide users to next steps

#### Overview/About Sections
- Provide comprehensive introductions to fostering in the location
- Include relevant statistics and key information
- Explain the local fostering landscape

#### Benefits/Support Sections
- Detail specific financial allowances available
- Explain support services and resources
- Include information about training and development opportunities

#### FAQ Sections
- Answer common questions about the fostering process
- Include location-specific requirements and procedures
- Provide clear, actionable information

#### Testimonials
- Share real experiences from local foster carers
- Include diverse perspectives and experiences
- Focus on the positive impact of fostering

## Field Types

### Text Fields
- Single-line text input for titles, names, and short descriptions

### Textarea Fields
- Multi-line text input for longer content
- Use for body text, descriptions, and answers

### Object Fields
- Grouped fields that belong together
- Example: CTA buttons with text and link fields

### Array Fields
- Repeatable sections for multiple items
- Example: FAQ items, resource lists, agency listings

## Best Practices

1. **Consistency** - Maintain consistent terminology and formatting
2. **Accessibility** - Use clear language and proper heading structure
3. **SEO** - Include relevant keywords naturally in content
4. **Local Focus** - Emphasize location-specific information
5. **User-Centered** - Focus on the needs of prospective foster carers
6. **Actionable** - Provide clear next steps and contact information
